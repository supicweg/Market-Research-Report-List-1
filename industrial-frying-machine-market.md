<p><ul><li>Batch Fryers</li><li>Continuous Fryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial frying machine market is primarily divided into two types: batch fryers and continuous fryers. Batch fryers are designed for smaller production needs, operating by frying a predetermined quantity of food in cycles. They are ideal for intermittent production and offer versatility for various recipes. In contrast, continuous fryers are built for high-volume operations, where food is constantly fed through the frying chamber, ensuring consistent quality and efficiency. Both types cater to different production scales and operational demands in the food industry.</p></p>
