<p><p>The photomask pellicle market is segmented by transmittance percentages, which indicate the amount of light that passes through the pellicle. Pellicles with ≥90% transmittance are ideal for high-resolution applications, ensuring minimal impact on the photolithography process. Those with 85%~90% transmittance offer a balance between cost and performance. Pellicles with 80%~85% are used in less critical applications, while those with ≤80% transmittance are generally less desirable due to higher light obstruction, impacting image quality and resolution in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
