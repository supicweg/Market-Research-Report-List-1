<p><p>The foldable phone market can be divided into two primary types: in-folding and out-folding devices. In-folding phones have screens that fold inward, offering a compact design that protects the display when closed; they typically have a smartphone exterior and open up to reveal a larger screen. Conversely, out-folding phones have screens that fold outward, allowing users to access a larger display without unfolding the device. Each type caters to different user preferences for portability and screen real estate.</p></p>
