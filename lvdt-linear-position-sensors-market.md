<p><p>LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) position sensors are essential in various industrial applications for precise measurement of linear displacement. The market is segmented into AC and DC types. AC type LVDTs operate using alternating current to generate a differential signal, known for their robustness and high accuracy, suitable for dynamic applications. Conversely, DC type LVDTs utilize direct current, offering simpler integration and signal processing, making them ideal for low-speed applications. Both types cater to diverse needs in automation and control systems.</p></p>
