<p><p>The chemical-resistant elastomer market comprises three main types: rubber, plastic, and silica gel. Rubber elastomers, like nitrile and EPDM, offer excellent flexibility and durability, making them suitable for harsh chemical environments. Plastic elastomers, such as thermoplastic elastomers (TPE), combine the properties of rubber and plastic, providing chemical resistance and processability. Silica gel elastomers are known for their high thermal stability and resistance to extreme conditions, making them ideal for specialized applications in chemical processing and storage.</p></p>
