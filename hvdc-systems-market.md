<p><p>HVDC (High Voltage Direct Current) systems are categorized into three main market types: </p><p>1. **Point-to-Point Transmission**: Direct connection between two locations, ideal for long-distance power transfer with minimal losses.</p><p>2. **Back-to-Back Station**: Connects two AC systems, facilitating stability and control by converting DC back to AC, often used in asynchronous grids.</p><p>3. **Multi-Terminal Systems**: Connects multiple power sources or grids, enhancing flexibility and efficiency in energy distribution, enabling integration of renewable sources and improving reliability.</p></p>
