<p><p>The modular roof deck systems market is primarily divided into two types: adjustable pedestal and fixed systems. Adjustable pedestal systems offer versatility by allowing for height customization to accommodate varying rooftop surfaces and drainage requirements, making them ideal for uneven terrains. In contrast, fixed systems provide a stable, permanent solution with predetermined height levels, ensuring consistent drainage and structural integrity. Both types are designed to enhance rooftop spaces, supporting weight-bearing needs and promoting efficient water management in urban environments.</p></p>
