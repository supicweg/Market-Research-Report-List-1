<p><strong>The Artificial Retinal Implants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Epiretinal Implants</li><li>Subretinal Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The artificial retinal implants market consists of two main types: epiretinal implants and subretinal implants. Epiretinal implants are positioned on the surface of the retina, stimulating retinal ganglion cells directly, which can help restore vision in patients with retinal diseases. In contrast, subretinal implants are placed beneath the retina, targeting the photoreceptor cells to enhance vision recovery. Both types aim to provide solutions for individuals with retinal degeneration, improving quality of life through advanced visual technology.</p></p>
