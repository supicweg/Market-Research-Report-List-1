<p><ul><li>Low Reflectance Chrome-film Mask Blanks</li><li>Attenuated Phase Shift Mask Blanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mask blanks are critical components in photolithography for semiconductor manufacturing, where they create patterns on silicon wafers. Low Reflectance Chrome-film Mask Blanks are designed to minimize light reflection, enhancing image quality and pattern fidelity during exposure. Attenuated Phase Shift Mask Blanks utilize phase-shifting techniques to improve resolution and contrast in printed features, essential for advanced node fabrication. Both types are crucial for achieving higher precision and efficiency in the production of integrated circuits, particularly as technology scales down.</p></p>
