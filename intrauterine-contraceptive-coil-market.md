<p><strong>The Intrauterine Contraceptive Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hormonal Intrauterine Device</li><li>Copper Intrauterine Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intrauterine contraceptive coil market comprises mainly two types: hormonal intrauterine devices (IUDs) and copper IUDs. Hormonal IUDs release progestin to prevent ovulation and thicken cervical mucus, offering effective contraception for several years. In contrast, copper IUDs utilize copper’s spermicidal properties to inhibit sperm movement and prevent fertilization. Both types provide long-term, reversible contraception options, catering to diverse preferences and medical needs, with varying side effects and efficacy rates influencing consumer choices in family planning.</p></p>
