<p><ul><li>Left Regular Lay</li><li>Left Lang Lay</li><li>Right Regular Lay</li><li>Right Lang Lay</li><li>Alternate Lay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel cable market can be classified into several types based on their lay orientation. Left Regular Lay cables have strands twisted to the left in a regular pattern, while Left Lang Lay cables feature a left twist with longer helical strands. Right Regular Lay cables twist to the right, adopting a similar regular structure. Right Lang Lay cables, conversely, employ a right twist with longer strands. Alternate Lay cables combine both twisting directions, enhancing flexibility and strength for varied applications.</p></p>
