<p><p>The industrial remote control market is mainly divided into two types: wireless and corded remote controls. Wireless remote controls utilize radio frequency, Bluetooth, or infrared technology, offering flexibility and ease of movement for users in various applications. They are ideal for situations where mobility is essential. In contrast, corded remote controls provide a direct physical connection to machinery, ensuring reliable control with minimal interference. This type is often preferred in environments where stability and lower latency are prioritized.</p></p>
