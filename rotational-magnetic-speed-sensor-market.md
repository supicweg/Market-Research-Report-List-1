<p><p>Rotational Magnetic Speed Sensors are categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact sensors. Non-contact sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect rotational speed without physical contact with the rotating element, providing durability and minimal wear. In contrast, contact sensors require direct interaction with the rotating component, offering high precision but potentially experiencing wear and requiring more maintenance. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including automotive and industrial applications, ensuring accurate monitoring of rotational speeds.</p></p>
