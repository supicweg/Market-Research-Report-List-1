<p><p>The Dynamic Water Cutting Machine market primarily comprises two types: sandless cutting and abrasive cutting. Sandless cutting employs high-pressure water jets to slice through materials without the use of abrasives, making it ideal for softer materials and reducing waste. In contrast, abrasive cutting incorporates fine particles mixed with water to enhance cutting efficiency, particularly for harder substances like metals and stones. Both types promote precision and versatility in various industrial applications, catering to diverse material processing needs.</p></p>
