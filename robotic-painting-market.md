In contrast, multi-axis robots feature multiple degrees of freedom, allowing for complex movements and the ability to handle intricate painting tasks on diverse surfaces. Multi-axis robots excel in precision and versatility, making them ideal for industries requiring high-quality finishes and adaptability to various product shapes and sizes.</p></p>
