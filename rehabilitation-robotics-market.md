<p><p>The rehabilitation robotics market primarily consists of three types: lower extremity devices, upper extremity devices, and exoskeletons. Lower extremity rehabilitation robots focus on aiding recovery of the legs and feet, often benefiting stroke or injury patients. Upper extremity robots assist in the rehabilitation of arms and hands, helping users regain mobility and strength. Exoskeletons are wearable robotic structures that support overall body movement, enhancing physical capabilities for rehabilitation, particularly for those with severe mobility impairments.</p></p>
