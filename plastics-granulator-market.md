<p><strong>The Plastics Granulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Beside-the-press Granulators</li><li>Central Granulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastics granulator market features two main types: beside-the-press granulators and central granulators. Beside-the-press granulators are compact and positioned close to injection molding machines, efficiently processing sprues and trims. They optimize production by enabling immediate recycling of plastic waste. Central granulators, on the other hand, are larger and centrally located, serving multiple machines. They handle higher volumes of scrap and often include advanced features for enhanced efficiency and processing capabilities, catering to larger production environments.</p></p>
