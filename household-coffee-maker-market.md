<p><p>The household coffee maker market comprises three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Semi-automatic models automate certain processes, like water heating, while still requiring user input for grinding and brewing, striking a balance between convenience and control. Fully automatic machines handle all aspects of coffee preparation—from grinding to brewing—at the touch of a button, appealing to consumers seeking maximum convenience and consistency.</p></p>
