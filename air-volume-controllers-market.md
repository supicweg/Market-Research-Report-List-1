<p><strong>The Air Volume Controllers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Variable Air Volume Controller</li><li>Constant Air Volume Controller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air volume controllers regulate the airflow in HVAC systems, ensuring efficient temperature control and energy use. The Variable Air Volume (VAV) controller adjusts airflow based on the heating or cooling demands of a space, optimizing energy efficiency while maintaining comfort. In contrast, Constant Air Volume (CAV) controllers maintain a fixed airflow rate, delivering consistent ventilation regardless of changing temperature needs. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing indoor air quality and providing energy-efficient climate control in commercial and residential settings.</p></p>
