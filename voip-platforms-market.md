<p><p>VoIP platforms are categorized into two main market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise VoIP solutions require businesses to install and maintain hardware and software on their premises, offering greater control and customization but necessitating significant upfront investment and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based VoIP solutions are hosted off-site by service providers, enabling businesses to access telephony services over the internet. This model reduces costs, simplifies management, and allows for greater scalability and flexibility in operation.</p></p>
