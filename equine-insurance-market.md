<p><p>Equine insurance encompasses various market types tailored to horse owners' needs. **Lifetime cover** provides coverage for serious health issues throughout the horse's life, ensuring ongoing treatment. **Non-lifetime cover** offers protection for a limited period or specific conditions, ceasing once the horse reaches a certain age or condition. **Accident-only insurance** covers injuries resulting from accidents but excludes illnesses. **Other market options** may include mortality insurance, which covers loss due to death, or specific policies for breeding horses, tailored to unique risks.</p></p>
