<p><p>Mainframe monitoring tools are classified into three main types based on their operational timing. Real-time monitoring tools provide immediate visibility into system performance, allowing for instant troubleshooting and alerting. Near-time monitoring tools analyze data with minimal delay, offering insights shortly after the data is collected, which aids in timely decision-making. Post-processing tools, on the other hand, evaluate historical data after events occur, helping organizations analyze trends, performance issues, and resource utilization for future optimization.</p></p>
