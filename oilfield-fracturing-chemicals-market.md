<p><p>The oilfield fracturing chemicals market includes various specialized products essential for enhancing hydraulic fracturing processes. Clear brine fluids provide density and stability, while shale inhibitors prevent clay swelling and ensure wellbore integrity. Lubricants reduce friction for improved fluid flow, and non-emulsifiers enhance separation of oil and water phases. H2S scavengers mitigate hazardous hydrogen sulfide gas, whereas defoamers control foam formation for smoother operations. Surfactants improve fluid performance by enhancing wetting and reducing surface tension, optimizing fracturing efficiency.</p></p>
