<p><p>The glueless laminate flooring market is divided into two main types: single layer flooring and multilayer flooring. In contrast, multilayer flooring consists of multiple layers, enhancing durability, insulation, and stability. This type often includes a core layer that improves sound and impact resistance, making it suitable for diverse environments. Both types utilize click-lock systems for easy, adhesive-free installation, catering to various consumer preferences.</p></p>
