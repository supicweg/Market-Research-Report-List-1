<p><strong>The Custom LASIK Surgery Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wavefront Guided</li><li>Wavefront Optimized</li><li>Topography LASIK</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Custom LASIK surgery market encompasses three primary types: Wavefront Guided, Wavefront Optimized, and Topography LASIK. Wavefront Guided LASIK utilizes detailed corneal mapping to create a personalized treatment plan, addressing higher-order aberrations for enhanced visual quality. Wavefront Optimized LASIK focuses on preserving the natural corneal shape to maintain better peripheral vision. Topography LASIK employs a comprehensive mapping of the cornea’s surface, ensuring precise adjustments for patients with irregular astigmatism. These advanced techniques collectively improve surgical outcomes and patient satisfaction.</p></p>
