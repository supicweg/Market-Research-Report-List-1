<p><p>The HEPA-ULPA filter market for cleanrooms is divided into two main categories: HEPA filters and ULPA filters. HEPA (High Efficiency Particulate Air) filters are designed to capture 99.97% of particles as small as 0.3 microns, making them ideal for many cleanroom applications. ULPA (Ultra Low Penetration Air) filters offer even greater efficiency, capturing 99.999% of particles down to 0.12 microns. Together, these filters play a crucial role in maintaining stringent air quality standards in controlled environments, such as laboratories and semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
