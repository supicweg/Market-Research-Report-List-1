<p><ul><li>Low Beam Lights</li><li>High Beam Lights</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive halogen headlights market comprises two main types: low beam lights and high beam lights. Low beam lights are designed for regular driving conditions, providing adequate illumination without blinding oncoming drivers, and are typically used during nighttime and in adverse weather. High beam lights, on the other hand, offer a more intense and focused light output, suitable for dark roads where no other vehicles are present, enhancing visibility significantly. Together, these types contribute to safer driving experiences.</p></p>
