<p><strong>The Smart Food Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RFID</li><li>Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Food Label Market includes two primary types: RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) and sensing technologies. RFID uses electromagnetic fields to automatically identify and track tags attached to food products, enhancing inventory management and reducing waste. Sensing technologies involve the use of biosensors or indicators that provide real-time data on food quality, freshness, and safety. Together, these technologies improve supply chain transparency, enhance consumer awareness, and promote food safety by allowing better monitoring of food conditions throughout the distribution process.</p></p>
