<p><p>The power battery market encompasses various materials critical for battery performance. Cathode materials, such as lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide (NMC) and lithium iron phosphate (LFP), influence energy density and stability. Anode materials, primarily graphite and silicon-based compounds, impact charging speed and capacity. Lithium-ion battery separators ensure safety and efficiency by preventing short circuits while allowing ion flow. Electrolytes, which can be liquid, gel, or solid-state, facilitate ion movement between electrodes, significantly affecting the battery's overall performance and lifespan.</p></p>
