<p><p>Nitrapyrin is a chemical compound widely used as a nitrification inhibitor in agriculture. It slows down the conversion of ammonium to nitrate in soil, thereby reducing nitrogen loss through leaching and denitrification. This helps improve nitrogen use efficiency, enhances crop yields, and minimizes environmental pollution caused by excess nitrogen runoff.</p><p>The Nitrapyrin Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.5% during the forecast period.
