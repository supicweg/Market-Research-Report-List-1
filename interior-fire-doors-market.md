<p><p>The interior fire doors market is primarily categorized into single leaf and double leaf doors. Single leaf doors consist of a single panel and are ideal for compact spaces, offering effective fire resistance while easing access. In contrast, double leaf doors feature two panels that can open together, facilitating wider openings for high-traffic areas or larger items. Both types are crucial for maintaining building safety standards, ensuring compartmentalization during a fire, and providing safe egress for occupants.</p></p>
