<p><strong>The Electric Dryers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vented Electric Dryers</li><li>Ventless/Condenser Dryers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric dryers market encompasses primarily two types: vented electric dryers and ventless/condenser dryers. Vented electric dryers expel moisture and hot air outside via a duct, making them efficient and quick for drying clothes. They typically require more installation space due to venting. On the other hand, ventless or condenser dryers recycle air and use a heat exchanger to condense moisture, allowing for flexible installation without external vents, making them ideal for apartments or smaller spaces.</p></p>
