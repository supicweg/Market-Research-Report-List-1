<p><strong>The Tile Levelling Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wedge System</li><li>Screw Cap Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tile leveling kits are crucial for ensuring even and precise tile installations. The market primarily features two types: wedge systems and screw cap systems. Wedge systems use wedges and spacers to secure tiles at uniform heights, preventing shifting during adhesive curing. Screw cap systems, on the other hand, utilize a threaded cap to adjust the height of tiles, allowing for fine-tuning and a more stable base. Both systems enhance tile alignment, reduce lippage, and improve the overall aesthetic of tiled surfaces.</p></p>
