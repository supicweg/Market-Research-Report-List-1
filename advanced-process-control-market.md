<p><p>The Advanced Process Control (APC) market encompasses various specialized control strategies to optimize industrial processes. Advanced Regulatory Control focuses on maintaining system stability using real-time feedback. Multivariable Model Predictive Control leverages complex models to forecast and manage multiple interacting process variables. Inferential Control provides estimates of unmeasurable process variables using measurable ones. Sequential Control manages processes in stages to optimize workflow, while Compressor Control specifically optimizes the performance and efficiency of compressors in various industrial applications.</p></p>
