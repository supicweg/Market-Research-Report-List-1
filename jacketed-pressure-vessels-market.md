<p><p>The jacketed pressure vessels market includes various types designed for thermal management. Conventional vessel jackets surround the vessel with a uniform space for heating or cooling fluids, ensuring even temperature distribution. Dimple vessel jackets feature a series of dimples that create efficient heat transfer while minimizing metal usage. Half pipe coil vessel jackets use curved pipes welded to the vessel surface, allowing precise control of thermal activity. Each type offers unique benefits for industrial applications, enhancing energy efficiency and process control.</p></p>
