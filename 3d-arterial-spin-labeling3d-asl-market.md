The two primary market types are Continuous Arterial Spin Labeling (CASL) and Pulsed Arterial Spin Labeling (PASL). CASL provides continuous labeling for improved signal stability, while PASL uses short RF pulses for labeling, offering flexibility in imaging protocols. Both techniques are valuable for assessing brain perfusion in various neurological conditions, enhancing diagnosis and treatment monitoring.</p></p>
