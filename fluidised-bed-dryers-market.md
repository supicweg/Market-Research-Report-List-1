<p><p>Fluidized bed dryers are crucial in processing industries, with two primary market types: batch type and continuous type. Batch type dryers process discrete quantities of material in separate cycles, allowing for flexibility in product variations and easier cleaning. In contrast, continuous type dryers operate with a steady flow of material, offering higher efficiency and consistent output, making them ideal for large-scale production. Both types serve various applications, balancing flexibility and efficiency based on operational needs.</p></p>
