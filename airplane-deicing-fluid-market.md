<p><p>The airplane deicing fluid market primarily utilizes two types of fluids: ethylene glycol and propylene glycol. Ethylene glycol-based fluids are highly effective at low temperatures, offering rapid ice and snow removal, but are more toxic and environmentally harmful. Propylene glycol-based fluids, while less toxic and more eco-friendly, are slightly less effective in extreme cold. Both fluids are diluted with water and additives to enhance performance and safety, catering to different operational and environmental requirements in aviation deicing processes.</p></p>
