<p><p>The active digitizer market is categorized into two main types: digital output and analog output. Digital output digitizers convert physical data into a digital format, enabling precise measurements and integration with computer systems for further analysis. In contrast, analog output digitizers generate continuous signals that represent the detected data, allowing for real-time monitoring and direct interaction with other analog devices. Both types are crucial in various applications, including industrial automation, medical devices, and consumer electronics, providing versatile solutions for data representation and processing.</p></p>
