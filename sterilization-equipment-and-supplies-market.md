<p><p>The sterilization equipment and supplies market offers various methods including thermosterilization, steam sterilization, and microwave sterilization. Thermosterilization utilizes heat to eliminate microorganisms, ensuring safety in medical and food applications. Steam sterilization, a widely accepted method, employs high-pressure steam to achieve effective germicidal action. Microwave sterilization leverages electromagnetic waves to generate heat within the materials, rapidly killing pathogens. Each method has unique advantages, catering to diverse industries seeking reliable sterilization solutions for enhanced safety and compliance.</p></p>
