<p><p>The cementitious tile adhesive market consists of two main types: ordinary tile adhesive and enhanced tile adhesive. Ordinary tile adhesive is a basic formulation suitable for standard tile installations, offering reliable bonding for various substrates. In contrast, enhanced tile adhesive incorporates additives that improve performance characteristics such as water resistance, flexibility, and setting time, making it ideal for more demanding applications like wet areas or heavy tile formats. Both types cater to different installation needs in the construction industry.</p></p>
