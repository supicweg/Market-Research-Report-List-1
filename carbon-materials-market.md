<p><p>The carbon materials market includes Carbon Nanotubes (CNTs), Carbon Nanofibers (CNFs), and Nano Carbon Balls (NCBs). CNTs are cylindrical nanostructures with exceptional strength, conductivity, and thermal properties, used in electronics, composites, and energy storage. CNFs are similar but with a fibrous structure, ideal for reinforcement in polymers and filtration. NCBs, also known as carbon onions, are spherical nanoparticles with high surface area, used in lubricants, coatings, and biomedical applications. Each material offers unique properties, driving demand across industries like aerospace, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
