<p><p>The contact measuring probe market comprises two primary types: machine tool probes and coordinate measuring machine (CMM) probes. Machine tool probes are utilized in CNC machines for in-process measurement and quality control, enhancing manufacturing efficiency. CMM probes, on the other hand, are designed for precise dimensional analysis of complex parts and assemblies, utilizing a coordinate measuring system for high accuracy. Both types are integral to ensuring product quality and precision in various manufacturing processes across industries.</p></p>
