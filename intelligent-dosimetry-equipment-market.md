<p><p>The Intelligent Dosimetry Equipment market encompasses advanced technologies like Thermoluminescent Dosimetry (TLD) and Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL). TLD involves using materials that store energy from ionizing radiation, which is released as light when heated, providing dose measurements. Conversely, OSL utilizes specific luminescent materials that emit light when stimulated by optical excitation, enabling real-time dosimetry. Both methods offer precise radiation monitoring, crucial for ensuring safety in medical, industrial, and research applications, thus driving market growth and innovation.</p></p>
