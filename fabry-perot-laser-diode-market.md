<p><p>The Fabry-Perot laser diode market is primarily categorized into two types: single mode and broadband. Single mode Fabry-Perot laser diodes emit light within a narrow wavelength spectrum, making them ideal for high-precision applications like telecommunications and sensing. In contrast, broadband Fabry-Perot laser diodes produce a wider range of wavelengths, suitable for applications such as optical coherence tomography and multi-wavelength systems. Each type serves distinct applications, with single mode focusing on efficiency and precision, while broadband emphasizes versatility and range.</p></p>
