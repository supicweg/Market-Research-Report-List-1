<p><strong>The Polyurethane Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thermoplastic Type</li><li>Thermosetting Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane systems are categorized into two main types: thermoplastic and thermosetting. Thermoplastic polyurethane (TPU) is characterized by its ability to be repeatedly melted and reshaped, offering flexibility and durability, making it ideal for applications in automotive, footwear, and coatings. In contrast, thermosetting polyurethane forms a rigid structure after curing, providing enhanced thermal and chemical resistance. This type is commonly used in foams, adhesives, and coatings, offering long-lasting performance for insulation and protective applications in various industries.</p></p>
