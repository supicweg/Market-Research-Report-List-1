<p><p>The glass laser engraving machine market comprises three main types: ultraviolet (UV) laser, CO2 laser, and green laser. UV lasers offer high precision and are ideal for intricate designs on various glass types. CO2 lasers are versatile and commonly used for deeper engravings, suitable for larger glass surfaces. Green lasers provide excellent absorption in glass, enabling high-quality markings and detailed engravings without significant thermal damage. Each type serves specific engraving needs, catering to different applications in the industry.</p></p>
