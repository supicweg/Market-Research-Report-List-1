<p><p>The Four Wheel Forklift market features two primary types: Internal Combustion Engine (ICE) and Electric forklifts. ICE forklifts are powered by gasoline or diesel, offering high power and extended operating hours, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications in outdoor environments. Electric forklifts, on the other hand, run on batteries, providing quieter, emissions-free operation ideal for indoor use and environments requiring strict safety protocols. Both types cater to different needs based on operational settings, costs, and sustainability preferences.</p></p>
