<p><strong>The Microneedles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hollow Microneedle Technology</li><li>Solid Microneedle Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microneedles market is primarily categorized into two types: hollow microneedles and solid microneedles. Hollow microneedle technology enables the delivery of fluids, such as vaccines or drugs, directly into the skin, enhancing therapeutic efficacy and minimizing pain. Conversely, solid microneedles are used for transdermal drug delivery through the skin's porous structure, often acting as a conduit for active ingredients without fluid transfer. Both technologies aim to improve drug delivery methods, ensuring patient comfort and compliance.</p></p>
