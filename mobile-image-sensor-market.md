<p><p>The mobile image sensor market encompasses two primary types: 2D and 3D sensors. 2D image sensors capture flat images by detecting light intensity across a planar surface, commonly used in smartphones and cameras for traditional photography. In contrast, 3D image sensors utilize depth perception techniques to create three-dimensional representations, enhancing augmented reality, facial recognition, and depth mapping applications. Both types contribute to advancing mobile technology, enabling enhanced user experiences and innovative features in various devices.</p></p>
