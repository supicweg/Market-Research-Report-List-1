<p><p>Bioanalytical testing services encompass the analysis of biological samples to measure small and large molecules, crucial for drug development and monitoring. Small molecules typically refer to low molecular weight compounds, including pharmaceuticals that can easily penetrate cell membranes. Large molecules, on the other hand, often include biologics like proteins, antibodies, and nucleic acids, which require more complex testing methods due to their size and structure. Both segments are essential for ensuring the safety and efficacy of therapeutics in clinical settings.</p></p>
