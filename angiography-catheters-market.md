<p><p>The angiography catheters market includes various types, each serving distinct purposes in vascular procedures. Scoring balloon catheters have specialized scoring mechanisms that allow for enhanced vessel dilation. Conventional catheters are widely used for standard angiographic procedures. Drug-eluting balloon (DEB) catheters deliver therapeutic agents to reduce restenosis. Cutting balloon catheters feature microscopic blades that incise plaque during dilation, promoting better outcomes. Together, these catheter types cater to diverse clinical needs in interventional cardiology and vascular therapies.</p></p>
