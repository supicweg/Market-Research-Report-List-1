<p><p>The industrial sterilizer market includes several types tailored for different applications. Steam sterilizers use heat and pressure to eliminate microorganisms rapidly. Hot air sterilizers utilize dry heat in a controlled environment. UV sterilizers harness ultraviolet light to disrupt microbial DNA. Ethylene oxide sterilizers employ gas for effective sterilization at low temperatures. Infrared sterilizers utilize infrared radiation for quick disinfection, while ozone sterilizers leverage ozone gas for its strong oxidizing properties. Each type addresses specific sterilization needs across various industries.</p></p>
