<p><p>The pH buffer solution market encompasses two main types: acidic and alkaline buffer solutions. Acidic buffer solutions maintain a pH below 7 and are commonly used in applications that require stabilization of lower pH levels, such as biological processes. Conversely, alkaline buffer solutions maintain a pH above 7, essential for applications requiring higher pH stability, such as biochemical assays. Both types are crucial in laboratories, industrial processes, and environmental monitoring, ensuring consistency and accuracy in pH-dependent reactions.</p></p>
